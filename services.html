    Frequently Asked Questions

    We've compiled answers to the most common questions clients ask about our technology strategy and consulting services.

    What types of technology consulting do you offer?

    We offer comprehensive technology strategy services including technology roadmaps, AI readiness assessments, cyber security evaluations, governance frameworks, capability assessments, and IT infrastructure reviews. Our services are designed for businesses of all sizes looking to align technology with business goals.

    How long does a typical technology assessment take?

    Most of our technology assessments range between 2 to 8 weeks, depending on the scope and complexity. For comprehensive technology strategy projects, we typically work over 6 to 12 weeks to ensure thorough analysis and practical recommendations.

    Do you help with AI implementation or just readiness assessment?

    We focus on strategic assessment and planning rather than hands-on implementation. Our AI readiness assessment helps you understand if your organization is ready for AI adoption, identifies high-value opportunities, and provides a roadmap for implementation. We work with your team to ensure successful execution.

    What's included in your technology strategy package?

    Our packages include executive interviews, current-state mapping, gap analysis, multi-year roadmaps with budgets, alternative scenarios, governance frameworks, and ongoing support. Each engagement is customized to your specific business needs and technology landscape.
